Alerts

Use the alert_box helper to display Bootstrap-styled alert messages.

Basic alerts

Use alert_box without options to render a basic alert box.

  
    <%= alert_box 'You accepted the Terms of service.' %>

generates the following HTML:

EXAMPLE
You accepted the Terms of service.
  
    <div class="alert alert-info" role="alert">You accepted the Terms of service.</div>

Dismissible alerts

Add dismiss: true to add a close button to the alert box.

  
    <%= alert_box 'You accepted the Terms of service.', dismiss: true %>

generates the following HTML:

EXAMPLE
You accepted the Terms of service. 
  
    <div class="alert alert-info" role="alert">
      <button class="close" data-dismiss="alert" type="button"><span aria-hidden="true">×</span></button>
       You accepted the Terms of service.
    </div>

Contextual alerts

Set the type option to change the color (and semantic context) of the alert message.

Available contexts are :success, :info (default), :warning and :danger.

  
    <%= alert_box 'You accepted the Terms of service.', type: :success %>

generates the following HTML:

EXAMPLE
You accepted the Terms of service. 
  
    <div class="alert alert-success" role="alert">You accepted the Terms of service.</div>

Complex alerts

To include HTML tags or a long text in the alert, pass your content in a block.

You can also specify custom options which will be added to the alert’s div tag.

  
    <%= alert_box type: :warning, dismiss: true, id: 'alert', class: :en, data: {js: 1} do %>
      <em>Thanks!</em> You accepted the <%= link_to 'Terms of service', '/' %>.
    <% end %>

generates the following HTML:

EXAMPLE
Thanks! You accepted the Terms of service. 
  
    <div id="alert" data-js="1" class="alert alert-warning en" role="alert">
      <button type="button" class="close" data-dismiss="alert" aria-label="Close"><span aria-hidden="true">×</span></button>
      <em>Thanks!</em> You accepted the <a href="/">Terms of service</a>.
    </div>
